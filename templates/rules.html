Game Rules

Game Includes:

  1. Trivia questions of various categories, including history, science, and sports
  2. Poker-style chips
  3. A form to input bets

To Win:

  1. Get as many questions right as possible, and bet as much as possible when you think you're right.
  2. Right answers are worth double your bet, so your chips can stack up quickly if you know your trivia.

Gameplay:

  1. Before the game begins, the player receives 100 game chips with which to bet.
  2. The minimum bet is always 10.
    • Even if a player loses all of their chips, they may always bet with this “free bet,” to keep them in the game and to add action to each question. Their chip stack will never drop below 10.
  3. Outside of playing the “free bet," there is no minimum bet, and each player can bet as much money as they have.
  4. When the trivia question pops up, the player submits their bet and clicks their answer.
  5. The correct answer is then revealed--green for the correct answer, red for all incorrect answers.
  6. Betting payouts:
    • 2:1 for getting the question correct.
  7. When the player bets incorrectly, they lose all the chips they bet.
  8. The game consists of 8 random questions.
  9. After the player finishes the game, the chips they've won are submitted for a high score.
  10. The chips the player's won are also added to their total stack.
