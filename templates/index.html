About the Game

Best Bet is going to look a little familiar to you if you've played some of the most popular trivia games. You can play the game--answering as many questions correctly as you can--but the real fun is in the gambling, betting on yourself to get questions right. That's where the points can really add up.

Bring your poker instincts. You don't even need to be the smartest trivia player to be the Best Bet. Good luck!



For Help
